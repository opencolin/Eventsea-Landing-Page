## 3. Copy critique on 3 events — rename + rewrite

The 3 events in the 41–48 range share a pattern. Templated descriptions, generic titles, weak topic-to-audience match. Here's what we'd ship instead.

### Event 11 — "GenAI Builders Meetup" — Austin

**Current title:** *GenAI Builders Meetup — Austin*
**Current description (excerpt):** "Join us for an evening of AI and networking with builders from across Texas. Drinks, demos, and great conversation."

**Recommended title:** *Production GenAI in Austin: 3 Companies Share What Broke in Their First 100 Customers*

**Recommended description:** "Three Austin teams — including [partner company A], [partner company B], and [partner company C] — present the specific things that broke when they took their GenAI feature to 100+ paying customers. Cost overruns, eval drift, latency cliffs. Bring questions; we're skipping the slides. Pitched at production engineers and senior PMs at AI-product companies."

**Estimated RSVP lift:** +24% (we model this at +60 → +75 expected RSVPs at the venue's historical conversion rate).
**Cost to ship the change:** 6 minutes.

### Event 12 — "AI for Enterprise Decision Makers" — NYC

**Current title:** *AI for Enterprise Decision Makers — NYC*
**Current description (excerpt):** "An evening of AI thought leadership for senior decision makers in the enterprise space."

The problem here is more structural than copy. The title attracts the audience that does not buy your product (senior buyers in non-technical lines of business). Of the 117 RSVPs we sampled, 71% had "VP" or higher in title and a non-engineering function. The event delivered the audience the title promised; that audience cannot purchase Acme.

**Recommended pivot (if repeated):** retarget at *technical* enterprise — staff engineers and engineering managers at F500 AI initiatives. Rename: *Inside the F500 AI Stack: 4 Staff Engineers on What Their CTOs Got Wrong.*

**Estimated RSVP lift:** the absolute RSVP count probably drops (smaller eligible audience), but qualified-pipeline lift is +200–300% based on role-mix delta. This is the highest-ROI rename in your slate.

### Event 13 — "AI Founders Connect" — SF

**Current title:** *AI Founders Connect — SF*
**Current description (excerpt):** "Connect with AI founders and operators in San Francisco. Co-hosted with [partner]."

**Recommended title:** *AI Founders Connect SF: Inference Cost-Per-Token Live Roast (Bring Your Bill)*

**Recommended description:** "Bring your last 30 days of inference bills. We'll walk through 3 of them on stage (anonymized) and the panel will roast cost lines, eval coverage, and routing choices. Pitched at technical founders running production AI products and the eng leaders one level down."

**Estimated RSVP lift:** +28%. The "bring your bill" mechanic over-indexes because it telegraphs the audience-pitch instantly.
**Cost to ship the change:** 8 minutes.
