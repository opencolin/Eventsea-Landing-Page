## Conventions

- **Buyer title** is the *first* person to write to. If that title doesn't exist at the account, fall back in order: DevRel lead → Field Marketing lead → Ecosystem/Partnerships lead → Head of Growth.
- **Budget signal** is a *plausible* hypothesis sourced from public conference sponsor lists, careers pages, and prior coverage. Confirm in discovery; do not quote back in cold touch.
- **Warm-intro path** is a *vector*, not a name. Founder fills real names in the per-account brief.
- **ICP-fit hypothesis** is the one sentence we'd open with if we only had one sentence.
