## What this is

Eventsea is a sponsorship intelligence layer for AI-infra and developer-tooling companies. We tell you **which 3–10 events next quarter are worth sponsoring** for your specific ICP, **deliver attribution** on the ones you fund, and **introduce you to the organizers** you should be talking to.

This pilot is a paid, time-boxed engagement to prove ROI on your next quarter of event spend. **Founder-delivered (white-glove) for the first cohort.**
